Rome, Italy

Rome is a city with nearly 3,000 years of influential art, architecture and culture. Ancient ruins including the Colosseum display the power of the former Roman Empire.

Sydney, Australia

Sydney is one of Australia's largest cities, and it's best known for its gorgeous harbours like the Massive Darling Harbour and the smaller Circular Quay port

Santorini, Greece

Santorini is one of of the Cyclades islands in the Aegean Sea. It was devastated by a volcanic eruption in the 16th BC, forever altering the landscape.

These are the cubiform houses from it's two principle towns, Fira and Oia, overlooking the sea.

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is known for its art and architecture. The modernist landmarks and churches feature modern art that dot the city.

Pictured here is the Sagrada Familia church, a large unfinished Roman Catholic church in Barcelona.

Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

Serengeti National Park is located in northern Tanzania, and is known for its massive annual migration of wildebeest and zebra. Many cross the crocodile-infested Grumeti River. Black rhinos inhabit the outcrops.

The Serengeti is the most renowned wildlife park in the entire world. The wilderness host great migration and boasts of large predators including lions, leopards, and cheatahs.
